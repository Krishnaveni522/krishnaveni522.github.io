SPECIAL FEATURES

The Perfect Blend of Modern Features
and the Classic Tale of Statesmanship


Selfie with Prime Minister

Take a selfie with your favourite Prime Minister using Augmented Reality.

Handwriting Robot

Instruct the Robot to create memorabilia just for you and
take home an inspiring quote with the signature of your
favourite Prime Minister.

Unity Wall

Stand shoulder to shoulder with fellow Indians and
celebrate our greatest strength - our unity - at the
Unity Wall.

Levitating Emblem & Kinetic Wings

The entrance is adorned with a 3D printed National
Emblem rotating in the air. Adding to the appeal is an
artistic mobile installation of multiple kinetic LED lights
forming a dynamic pattern of the National Flag
representing freedom, progress and growth of the nation.

Shatabdi Sankalp

Write a message on India of your dreams in 2047.

Stroll with Prime Ministers

An unforgettable stroll with the architects of Independent
India! This installation offers you the opportunity to walk
alongside your favourite Prime Minister in Augmented Reality.

( Please note: The installation uses ‘Chroma Key’
technology which does not capture green colour. )

Audio Guide

Explore the Pradhanmantri Sangrahalaya with an Audio Guide. Opt for the handheld digital device that helps you understand the exhibits.

Sketch and Bring a Mission to Life

Watch your kids bring their imagination to life. Join them in
learning about India's space programme and the successful
Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan missions.
